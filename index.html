Who We Are

A School That Forms the Whole Person

Anum Presbyterian Senior High School has stood as a beacon of learning, faith, and character formation in the Eastern Region of Ghana since its founding in 1937. What began as a vision of the Presbyterian Church of Ghana has grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions.

Our approach is holistic — academic excellence alone is not enough. ANSEC students leave not just with certificates, but with values, discipline, and a love of service that shapes their contributions to society for decades to come.

Presbyterian Foundation

Faith That Transforms, Not Just Informs

ANSEC was founded by the Presbyterian Church of Ghana on the conviction that education and faith are inseparable. More than eight decades later, that conviction still shapes every morning devotion, every act of service, and every relationship in this school community.

The chaplaincy, Scripture Union, and the rhythms of chapel worship are not additions to school life — they are the heartbeat of it. Students are not merely taught about faith; they are invited into it.
