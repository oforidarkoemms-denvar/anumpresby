Headteacher's Message

Mrs. Stella Naa Enyo Atiase

Head Teacher, ANSEC

Welcome to Anum Presbyterian Senior High School — an institution built on a foundation of faith, discipline, and an unwavering commitment to academic excellence. For over eight decades, ANSEC has stood as a beacon of purposeful education in the Eastern Region, and it is both a privilege and a profound responsibility to lead this great school.

Our mission is clear: to equip every student not only with knowledge, but with the character, moral resolve, and critical thinking necessary to serve Ghana and the world with distinction. We believe that excellence is not an event — it is a culture, patiently nurtured by dedicated teachers and embraced by students of ambition and integrity.

To our students, I say: the legacy of this institution is yours to build upon. To our teachers and staff, I say: your dedication is the very engine of ANSEC's greatness. Together, we press forward — rooted in history, rising in excellence.

Stella Naa Enyo Atiase
Head Teacher — Anum Presbyterian Senior High School

Senior Administration

Leading with Structure and Purpose

01
Assistant Headmaster — Academics
Mr. Emmanuel Akita

Oversees all academic programmes, curriculum planning, timetabling, examination coordination, and departmental performance. Works directly with Heads of Department to ensure academic standards and WAEC alignment across all subject areas.

02
Assistant Headmaster — Administrative Operations
Mr. Daniel K. Acquah

Responsible for the day-to-day administrative operations of the school, including staff welfare, institutional records management, procurement, community relations, and compliance with Ghana Education Service directives.

03
Assistant Headmaster — Domestic
Mrs. Abena Frimpomaa Agyei

Manages all domestic and residential affairs of the school, including boarding facilities, student welfare in dormitories, catering services, hygiene standards, and the overall physical environment of the campus.

04
Senior House Master
Mr. Kwabena Asumah Poku

Coordinates all male boarding houses and ensures discipline, welfare, and pastoral care for male students in residence. Supervises house masters and monitors conduct within the dormitory blocks throughout the academic year.

05
Senior House Mistress
Mrs. Esther Ababio Dewi

Oversees all female boarding houses with a focus on the welfare, discipline, emotional support, and academic engagement of female students in residence. Works closely with the Assistant Head (Domestic) on boarding policies and pastoral matters.

Every member of the ANSEC team — from the Headteacher to the most recent staff appointment — is bound by a shared covenant: to serve the students of this institution with discipline, compassion, and an uncompromising commitment to academic and moral excellence.

We measure our success not in examination scores alone, but in the character of every graduate who walks through our gates — equipped to serve Ghana and the world with distinction. The administration and staff of ANSEC stand united in this purpose, generation after generation.
