Campus Overview

A Campus Built for Growth

Set on a generous campus in Anum in the Eastern Region of Ghana, ANSEC's grounds have expanded steadily since 1937 — shaped by decades of institutional investment, community pride, and the conviction that a school's physical environment matters deeply to the quality of education it provides.

Today the campus encompasses academic blocks, boarding facilities, laboratories, a library, a chapel, sports grounds, and dedicated spaces for the arts — everything a serious senior high school needs, continuously being improved.

Residential Life

Boarding at ANSEC

Life at ANSEC is not confined to the classroom. Our boarding programme is one of the school's greatest strengths — providing a structured, supervised, and deeply communal residential experience that has shaped the character of generations of ANSEC students.

From evening prep to morning devotion, from housemaster guidance to inter-house competitions, boarding life at ANSEC is designed to build discipline, community, and resilience in every student who lives on campus.

Separate Houses
Male and female houses fully separated, each with dedicated housemaster or housemistress care.
Structured Schedule
Morning roll call, meals, prep periods, lights-out — a routine that builds self-discipline.
Pastoral Support
Resident staff available for counsel, welfare checks, and support throughout the term.
Inter-House Culture
A vibrant inter-house competition programme in sports, academics, and the arts fostering house pride.
Secure Compound
Gated, fenced, and supervised at all times — providing parents with confidence in student safety.
Prep & Study Time
Dedicated evening study hours with supervision ensuring boarders maintain academic momentum.

Sports & Recreation

ANSEC's sporting culture is one of the school's proudest traditions. From national-level athletics to inter-house football, sport at ANSEC is not optional — it is essential.

Football

ANSEC's most celebrated sport — with an active inter-house league, school team, and a proud history of regional competition.

Athletics

Track and field events across sprints, relays, long jump, and javelin — competing at zonal and regional championships.

Volleyball

Male and female teams competing in inter-school and regional volleyball tournaments throughout the academic year.

Basketball

A growing programme on our hard-surface courts with active participation from both male and female students.

Table Tennis

A popular indoor sport offering students year-round recreation and competitive inter-house and inter-school tournaments.

Handball

Competitive handball for both male and female students — a fast-growing sport in the ANSEC inter-house programme.

Cross Country

Annual cross-country races forming a cherished school tradition — testing endurance, grit, and house unity.

Swimming

Students participate in swimming competitions at inter-school galas, representing ANSEC in aquatic sport at regional level.

Past, Present & Future

Campus Development

ANSEC's campus has never stopped growing. Every decade has brought new buildings, improved facilities, and a renewed commitment to providing the environment our students deserve.

1937
The Founding Campus

ANSEC established on its current site in Anum with initial classroom blocks and residential accommodation — a modest but purposeful beginning for what would become one of the Eastern Region's leading secondary schools.

Completed
1960s – 1970s
Post-Independence Expansion

Significant campus development in the decades following Ghanaian independence — new academic blocks, additional dormitories, and the formalisation of the school's sports grounds and assembly spaces to meet a growing student body.

Completed
1980s – 1990s
Science & Library Infrastructure

Construction of dedicated science laboratories and a formalised school library — transforming ANSEC's academic infrastructure and enabling the school to fully meet the demands of the WAEC science syllabus at practical level.

Completed
2000s – 2010s
ICT Centre & Refurbishment

Establishment of the school's first dedicated ICT laboratory, major refurbishment of the dining hall and boarding facilities, resurfacing of sports grounds, and improvements to the chapel — bringing the campus into the modern era.

Completed
2020 – Present
Ongoing Improvement Programme

Active ongoing investment in classroom furnishing, ICT equipment upgrades, dormitory renovation, borehole water infrastructure, perimeter security, and solar power installation — supported by government grants, the PTA, and alumni contributions.

In Progress
Near Future
New Multi-Purpose Block

Planning is underway for a new multi-purpose block to house an expanded library, additional ICT suites, a conference room, and staff offices — consolidating ANSEC's academic resources in a single modern facility aligned with GES standards.
