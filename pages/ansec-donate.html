Why It Matters

A School That Forms the Whole Person

ANSEC does more than educate — it nurtures faith, builds character, and opens doors that would otherwise remain closed for many students from Anum and beyond.

"
Education is the greatest equaliser. At ANSEC, every donation helps a deserving student step into the future they were born for.
— ANSEC Alumni Association

Anum Presbyterian Senior High School has been a beacon of excellence for over eight decades. But sustaining that excellence requires more than government allocation — it requires the commitment of alumni, parents, the church, and friends who believe in the mission.

Your generosity directly funds the resources, spaces, and opportunities that help our students thrive — academically, spiritually, and as citizens.

Bursaries & Scholarships
Many brilliant students cannot afford fees. Gifts to the bursary fund keep deserving students in school and on the path to success.
Science & Technology Labs
Modern labs equipped with up-to-date instruments mean students graduate ready for university and the workforce.
Faith & Character Infrastructure
The chapel, chaplaincy programmes, and character formation activities form the spiritual backbone of the ANSEC experience.

A Student's Story

The Gift That Kept Me in School

"

I almost had to drop out in SHS 2. My mother had lost her job and there was simply no money for fees. Then the school's bursary committee came through — funded by people I had never met who believed in students like me. That gift didn't just keep me in school. It kept my dream alive. I am now in my first year at UG studying Medicine, and I owe it in part to those unnamed donors who decided a stranger's future was worth investing in.

AK
Abena Kyei
ANSEC Class of 2024 · University of Ghana, Medicine
