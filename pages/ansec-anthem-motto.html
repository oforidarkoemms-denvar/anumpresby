The School Anthem

Sung at every assembly, every prize-giving, and every occasion that calls us to remember who we are and what we stand for as a school and community.

I
Verse One
Praise the Lord who built this school for us, Set in Anum, by the Volta's shore; Here we learn of God and all His ways, Here we train for life and for His cause. Lift our voices, sing with joy and praise, ANSEC, our beloved school!
II
Verse Two
Here we seek for wisdom, truth, and light, Guided by the Word of God each day; May our hearts be pure and our hands strong, As we walk the straight and narrow way. Give us grace to live in peace and love, ANSEC, our guiding star!
Chorus
Knowledge, Discipline, and Service — These the words we proudly bear; In our hearts and in our living, May these virtues ever shine. ANSEC! ANSEC! Hear our voices ring, To our school our praises bring!
III
Verse Three
When the years have passed and we have gone, Far beyond these gates we love so well, May the flame that burned in us burn on, May the story of our deeds still tell. That we served with honour, faith, and grace, Children of this hallowed place!
IV
Verse Four
Lord, we bring this school before Your throne, Bless the teachers, bless the students here; Let Your peace and wisdom fill each heart, Let Your Spirit guide through every year. ANSEC stands by faith through storm and sun — God's own work, His will be done!

About the Anthem

More Than Words

The ANSEC anthem is not merely a ceremonial song. It is a declaration of identity — a statement of what this school believes, what it asks of its students, and what it offers the world through those who pass through its gates.

Sung since the school's earliest days, the anthem has accompanied generations of students through prize-givings, speech days, inter-school competitions, and quiet morning assemblies — its words woven into the fabric of each student's formation.

Sung at Every Assembly
The anthem opens formal school gatherings, uniting students, staff, and visitors under one shared identity.
Rooted in Presbyterian Tradition
Reflecting ANSEC's founding, the anthem is grounded in faith, scripture, and a sense of divine calling in education.
Embodies the Motto
Each verse of the anthem reinforces one of the three motto pillars — Knowledge, Discipline, and Service — giving the motto a living musical expression.
A Bond Across Generations
Alumni who graduated decades ago can still sing every word — the anthem is one of the most powerful ties binding the ANSEC community across time.
Two Words, One Purpose

Living the Motto

At ANSEC, the motto is not decorative — it is descriptive. These two words define the goal of every lesson taught, every rule upheld, and every life shaped within these walls.

Pillar One

Knowledge

"The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom." — Proverbs 9:10

At ANSEC, Knowledge means more than memorising facts. It means cultivating a genuine love of learning — an intellectual curiosity that drives students to ask deeper questions, seek truth with rigour, and remain open to discovery long after they leave school.

  • Academic excellence across WAEC subjects and all programmes
  • Library, laboratory, and ICT access to support research
  • A culture of questioning, debate, and intellectual engagement
  • Teachers who model lifelong learning in their own lives
Knowledge
Pillar Two

Discipline

"Whoever loves discipline loves knowledge." — Proverbs 12:1

Discipline at ANSEC is about character — the inner ordering of the self: the ability to persist when learning is hard, act with integrity when no one watches, and build habits that last. The school's boarding programme, daily schedule, and code of conduct nurture this discipline.

  • Structured boarding schedule: prep, devotion, roll calls
  • Code of conduct that fosters responsibility and accountability
  • Sports and physical education building mental toughness
  • House system fostering healthy competition and self-regulation
Discipline
Pillar Two

Service

"The greatest among you shall be your servant." — Matthew 23:11

Service is the outward expression of all that ANSEC builds inward. Every ANSEC student is called to use their knowledge and discipline not only for personal advancement, but for the benefit of others — family, community, Ghana, and beyond.

  • Community service integrated into school life and clubs
  • Leadership through the prefect body and house offices
  • Alumni serving in medicine, law, education, ministry, and governance
  • The Presbyterian ethos of servant leadership as the highest calling
Service
Our Identity

The anthem and motto of Anum Presbyterian Senior High School are not artefacts of the past. They are living statements — sung every morning, lived every day, carried into every classroom, every playing field, and every life that ANSEC touches.

We invite you to explore what it means to be part of a school community shaped by Knowledge, Discipline, and Service — and to discover how these three words have produced graduates making a difference across Ghana and beyond.
