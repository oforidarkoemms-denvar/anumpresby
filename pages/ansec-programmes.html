Academic Overview

Five Pathways to Excellence

At ANSEC, every programme is built on the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) framework, enriched by our Presbyterian heritage of faith, rigorous scholarship, and community service. Students follow a shared core curriculum alongside programme-specific electives, graduating with the knowledge and character to excel in Ghana's best universities — and in life.

Programme 01

General Science

"Inquiry, experimentation, and discovery — the language of the natural world."

The Science programme equips students with analytical thinking, laboratory skills, and a deep understanding of the physical and biological world. It is the gateway to medicine, engineering, pharmacy, environmental science, and technology. ANSEC's well-equipped science laboratories ensure every concept is grounded in hands-on experience.

Programme 05

Business Studies

"Commerce, enterprise, and leadership — building tomorrow's business minds."

The Business programme prepares students for Ghana's competitive commerce, finance, and entrepreneurship landscape. Through accounting, economics, cost management, and business mathematics, students develop financial literacy and strategic thinking. Graduates enter banking, accountancy, and marketing with a strong competitive edge.
