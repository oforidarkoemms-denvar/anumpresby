STEM Robotics Club

Engineering the Future

The STEM Robotics Club is ANSEC's most dynamic co-curricular programme. Students collaborate in teams to design and build working robots using motors, sensors, and microcontrollers — then programme them to complete real-world challenges.

From obstacle-course navigators to line-following machines, every project teaches computational thinking, teamwork under pressure, and the joy of making something work with your own hands.

Brass Band

ANSEC Brass Band

Sound That Moves a School

The ANSEC Brass Band is the heartbeat of every school parade, prize-giving ceremony, and public event. Founded as part of ANSEC's rich musical heritage, the band trains students in trumpet, trombone, French horn, tuba, and percussion — producing musicians who carry ANSEC's name far beyond the campus.

Band members receive formal instrumental coaching, develop ensemble discipline, and build an appreciation for music that will last a lifetime. Whether marching on the parade ground or performing at an inter-school festival, the Brass Band is ANSEC at its proudest.
