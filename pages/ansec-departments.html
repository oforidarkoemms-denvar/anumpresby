Our Pedagogy

How We Teach at ANSEC

Across all departments, our approach centres on four pillars that transform students from passive recipients into active, lifelong learners.

Inquiry-Based Learning

Students are encouraged to question, investigate, and discover — not merely memorise. Every department builds lessons around meaningful problems.

Collaborative Culture

Group projects, peer learning, and team-based tasks build communication and social skills alongside academic knowledge.

Continuous Assessment

Regular formative assessments ensure no student is left behind. Teachers identify gaps early and intervene with targeted support.

WASSCE Excellence

All teaching is aligned to GES curriculum outcomes and WASSCE objectives, ensuring students are thoroughly prepared for national examinations.
